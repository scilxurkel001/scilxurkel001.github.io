License and Legal Issues Hall of Shame Projects Using FFmpeg

Hall of Shame

This is a list of projects or companies violating FFmpeg's license. The list is part of an effort to get them to comply with the licensing terms by shaming them in public.

A good way to help us is to link to this page, thereby boosting its pagerank, possibly near or past the homepage of the offender. We discourage you from using or buying the software listed here, unless you do so to pester its producers and demand that they comply with the license of FFmpeg.

Our issue tracker also contains a list of license violations. If you become aware of license infringements, please notify us on the ffmpeg-devel mailing list and/or add it to our issue tracker.
